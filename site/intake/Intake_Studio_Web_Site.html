Web Site Intake

This page is focused on the structure and presentation of the site. The goal is to understand how many sections it needs, how content should be organized, and how visitors should move through the experience.

01 | Project BasicsCore identity and purpose
02 | Structure & NavigationMain sections, subsections, and hierarchy
How many main sections?Think Home, About, Services, Portfolio, Contact, Blog, FAQ, etc.
How many subsections?What needs to live under each major section?
Single page or multi-page?Clarify whether the site should scroll as one story or branch into multiple pages.
03 | Content RequirementsWhat content the site needs to communicate
04 | Conversion & Business GoalsWhat the site needs visitors to do
05 | Design DirectionBrand expression and visual style
This intake is intentionally more architectural. The goal is to lock the structure of the website early so content, design, and navigation all move in the same direction.
