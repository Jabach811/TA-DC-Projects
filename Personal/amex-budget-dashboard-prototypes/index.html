Amex Workbook Concepts

Five directions for the budget tracking app.

These concepts use the same Amex source snapshot: 824 transactions from January 1, 2022 to September 14, 2022, with $41,400.14 in charges, -$37,760.41 in payments and credits, and a net total of $3,639.73.

01
Muted Minimal
Quiet, premium, and finance-forward. Clean cards, soft lines, muted palette, and practical charts.
Good fit if you want calm and highly readable.

Glass Control
Dark glass dashboard with high-contrast metric tiles, ring gauges, and a more app-like command center feel.

Canvas Budget
Warm, creative, and visual. Uses boards, goal strips, and category color blocks to feel more like a planning studio.
